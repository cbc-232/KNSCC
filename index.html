    Services

    We offer at Koidu New Sembehun City Council (K.N.S.C.C).

    WASTE MANAGEMENT

    Our services include efficient collection and disposal systems for household and commercial waste, recycling programs aimed at reducing environmental impact, and initiatives for educating the community on responsible waste management practices. For more details on waste management services, please contact us through this website or come to our office directly.

    COMMUNITY FACILITIES

    Here are an array of facilities designed to enhance the quality of life for all residents. Public libraries with diverse books and resources, recreational centers featuring sports amenities and playgrounds, versatile community halls for events and meetings, and accessible health clinics ensuring the well-being of our community. contact us directly for more information.

    FEEDER ROADS

    K.N.S.C.C is dedicated to enhancing local infrastructure through the development and maintenance of feeder roads. These roads are vital for connecting communities, facilitating transportation, and supporting economic growth. We prioritize efficient planning & construction for safe and accessible feeder roads that benefit all residents. For inquiries, please contact us.

    CULVERT & BRIDGES

    K.N.S.C.C. offers services related to culverts and bridges. Recently, the City Council held a bid opening ceremony for the maintenance of selected feeder roads and the construction of culverts and a six-meter bridge within the municipality. These efforts aim to enhance transportation and connectivity for residents. For more information, please contact us directly.

    MUNICIPAL BYLAWS

    K.N.S.C.C. ensures community well-being through comprehensive municipal bylaws that cover areas such as noise control, waste management, and zoning regulations. We promote compliance through community engagement, ensuring a safe and harmonious environment for all residents. For more information or to report concerns, please contact us directly.

    DISPUTE RESOLUTION

    With all the bylaws being created, modfied and even enforced, there are always some people that will not sometimes get it right with their co-residents, K.N.S.C.C is good at handling such issues and has always been succesful at it. Discover how we can assist you in navigating disputes efficiently and peacefully. Feel free to contact us directly for more information or to report concerns.
